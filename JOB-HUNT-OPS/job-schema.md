## Scoring Rubric

| Factor | Weight | Criteria |
|--------|--------|----------|
| EEE Relevance | 30% | Direct EEE role = 100, adjacent (energy/power) = 70, general eng = 40 |
| Entry Level | 25% | Graduate/junior = 100, 0-2 yrs = 80, 2-5 yrs = 40, senior = 10 |
| Location | 20% | Nairobi = 100, Kenya = 70, Remote = 90, East Africa = 50 |
| Company Quality | 15% | Known company = 100, has website = 70, no web presence = 20 |
| Recency | 10% | < 3 days = 100, < 7 days = 70, < 14 days = 40, older = 10 |

**Thresholds:**
- Score > 90: 🔥 alert in #alerts
- Score > 70: post to #validated
- Score < 70: stays in #feed-eee only
- Score < 40: might be noise, log but don't post
