# Remote Work Platforms — Application Guides

Three skill-based remote platforms vetted for Kenya. All accept EE + Python backgrounds.

## The Three

| Platform | Pay Range | Best For | Kenya Status |
|----------|-----------|----------|--------------|
| [[Mercor — Application Guide]] | $50-210/hr | Highest ceiling, STEM/SWE | Eligible (bias toward US/UK) |
| [[Micro1 (STEM Expert) — Application Guide]] | $30-85/hr | STEM degree leverage | Confirmed |
| [[Andela — Application Guide]] | $3K-12K+/mo | Real engineering contracts | Home market |

## Strategy

1. **Apply to Mercor + Micro1 now** — faster onboarding, income while you wait
2. **Start Andela process in parallel** — takes longer but different category of reward
3. **Stack all three** — none are guaranteed steady income

## Key Differentiators

- **Mercor:** AI video interview, weekly Stripe payouts, highly selective
- **Micro1:** "Zara" AI interview (recorded), bi-monthly payments, STEM tier pays 2x generalist
- **Andela:** 3-stage technical process, monthly Deel payments, 12-month contracts, ~30-40% cut

## Common Thread

All three value:
- STEM degrees (EE counts)
- Python/coding skills
- Strong English communication
- Domain expertise over generalist work

## What NOT to Apply As

Don't apply as a generalist on any of these. Your EE + Python combo is the differentiator. Apply for coding/STEM roles specifically.
