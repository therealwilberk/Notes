# The meatseller

**Source:** Aeon  
**Author:** Aeon Video  
**Date:** 2026-05-27

---

In _The Meatseller_ , a young woman named Selinna Ajamikoko shares her harrowing, years-long migration from her home country of Nigeria to Italy, which she began aged just 15. Directed by the Italian animator Margherita Giusti, who met Ajamikoko in Rome through an organisation that supports asylum seekers and refugees, and produced by the acclaimed Italian director Luca Guadagnino, the film traces a path from Ajamikoko’s childhood desire to follow in her mother’s trade as a meatseller to the traumas she endured while trying to make that dream come true and support her family back home. Giusti brings both extraordinary sensitivity and visual invention to the story, as colours, styles and the extended metaphor of cattle and predatory animals shift throughout the work, reflecting the intense emotions at each stage of Ajamikoko’s perilous journey.

Director: [Margherita Giusti](https://www.margheritagiusti.com/)

Producer: Luca Guadagnino
