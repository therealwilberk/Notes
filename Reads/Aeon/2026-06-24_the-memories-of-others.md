# The memories of others

**Source:** Aeon  
**Author:** Aeon Video  
**Date:** 2026-06-24  
**Link:** https://aeon.co/videos/a-japanese-photographers-striking-perceptive-images-of-the-troubles

---

Born into an elite Japanese family, the photographer Akihiko Okamura (1929-85) was an unlikely candidate to become one of the most perceptive chroniclers of the Troubles in Northern Ireland. A collaboration between Photo Museum Ireland and the Akihiko Okamura Archive, _The Memories of Others_ traces how the photographer came to create original and thoughtful depictions of the decades-long sectarian conflict, many of which remained largely unseen until recently. Combining archival footage, interviews and Okamura’s photographs, the short documentary reveals how a deeply rooted sense of justice led him to embrace empathy and eschew spectacle, resulting in images that juxtapose everyday life with extraordinary violence.
