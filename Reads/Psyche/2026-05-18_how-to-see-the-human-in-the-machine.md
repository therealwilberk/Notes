# How to see the human in the machine

**Source:** Psyche  
**Author:** Psyche Video  
**Date:** 2026-05-18  
**Link:** https://psyche.co/videos/the-ai-age-is-inevitable-lets-make-sure-its-human-centred

---

Merely seeing the word ‘AI’ can be enough to make some people recoil – doubly so if it’s paired with the word ‘art’. But, as the thinkers in this video from the Museum of Modern Art (MoMA) in New York argue, if artists and other critical thinkers sit out this potentially ‘civilisational moment’, we’ll end up with an AI paradigm that reflects the interests of powerful governments and corporations rather than our humanity. In doing so, we may amplify the missteps that have led to today’s advertisement-saturated and slop-suffused internet. In particular, the video explores the work of the Berlin-based artists Holly Herndon and Mat Dryhurst, whose experiments in AI centre the presence, voice and consent of humans, as well as the work of Fei-Fei Li, the co-director of Stanford University’s Human-Centered AI Institute. The wide-ranging exploration that emerges argues that AI systems, and the datasets that shape them, must be approached with creativity and criticism rather than written off or ignored.
