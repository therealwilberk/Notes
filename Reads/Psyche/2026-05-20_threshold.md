‘Let it be like a lullaby that holds you.’

Threshold Choir is an international charity that provides people who are dying with soothing, non-religious songs of peace and comfort, sung by three-person volunteer groups. This short documentary profiles one Threshold Choir chapter in Devon in south-west England. The group is led by Nickie Aven who, having lost her husband and son in quick succession, is no stranger to death and grief. The director Florence Browne channels the group’s gentle kindness in her film, which captures both the comfort the choir members provide and how spending time close to death has changed their perspectives on its inevitability and many mysteries.
