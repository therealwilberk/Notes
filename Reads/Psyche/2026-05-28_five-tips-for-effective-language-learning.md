# Five tips for effective language learning

**Source:** Psyche  
**Author:** Psyche Video  
**Date:** 2026-05-28  
**Link:** https://psyche.co/videos/go-beyond-the-apps-to-build-language-learning-skills-that-stick

Your favourite gamified language-learning app might offer fun ways to expand your vocabulary and drill down on grammar rules. However, more focused strategies can be helpful for language learners looking to make the big leap from quizzing in the comfort of home to speaking out in the world. In this short video from BBC Ideas and the Open University, the multilingual UK writer Sophia Smith Galer breaks down five nifty tips for more effective language learning aimed at making real conversations in your target language less timid and more natural. For a deeper dive into learning a language outside the classroom – and sticking with it – read this [Psyche Guide](https://psyche.co/guides/how-to-approach-the-lifelong-project-of-language-learning).
