# The keeper

After a series of odd jobs, Stacey Vazquez settled on a surprising career for any New Yorker: professional beekeeper. It is an occupation that is perhaps doubly unexpected for Vazquez, who has a potentially deadly allergy to thousands of her co-workers. The short film _The Keeper_ captures Vazquez explaining why she loves the job despite its strenuous nature and the risks it poses. This includes the lessons the bees teach her about cooperation and the joy she finds in sharing the work with fellow New Yorkers, who often lack access to the natural world. For his stylish short, the filmmaker Skyler Knutzen pairs a fluttering piano score with shots of Vazquez working on the rooftop gardens of skyscrapers, creating a striking juxtaposition between these small, green slices of the city and the endless sea of glass and steel beyond them.
