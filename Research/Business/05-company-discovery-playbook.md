## 1. GOVERNMENT REGISTRIES & DATABASES

### 1.1 EPRA — Energy and Petroleum Regulatory Authority
- **Main site:** https://www.epra.go.ke
- **Renewable Energy Registers:** https://www.epra.go.ke/renewable-energy-registers
  - Lists licensed renewable energy projects and companies
- **Energy Efficiency Registers:** https://www.epra.go.ke/index.php/energy-efficiency-registers
  - Registered energy auditors and firms
- **Licensed Electrical Contractors (direct PDF):**  
  - https://www.eeekenya.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/12/REGISTER-OF-LICENSED-ELECTRICAL-CONTRACTORS-AS-AT-5th-FEBRUARY-2021.pdf
  - Updated register of all EPRA-licensed electrical contractors (Class A, B, C) with company names, license numbers, phone numbers, emails, and locations
- **Electricity licensing page:** https://www.epra.go.ke/electricity
  - Issuing, renewing, modifying, suspending or revoking licenses

**Action:** Download the contractor register PDF — it has emails and phone numbers directly. Search the renewable energy registers for solar/wind/hydro project developers.

### 1.2 NCA — National Construction Authority
- **Main site:** https://www.nca.go.ke
- **Search Registered Contractors:** https://www.nca.go.ke/registered-contractors
  - Searchable database of registered contractors by category (including electrical engineering works)
- **Local Contractors Registration:** https://www.nca.go.ke/local-contractors
  - Categories include electrical engineering works (requires EPRA license)

**Action:** Use the search tool to find contractors registered for electrical engineering works. Filter by class and location. Cross-reference with EPRA license numbers.

### 1.3 PPRA — Public Procurement Regulatory Authority
- **Main site:** https://ppra.go.ke
- **Contract Awards:** https://ppra.go.ke/contract-awards/
  - Lists all public procurement contract awards including tender notices, awarded contracts, suppliers and their directors
  - Required by law to publish all procurement information

**Action:** Search contract awards for electrical/solar/energy projects. You'll find companies that WON government tenders — these are active, revenue-generating firms. Each listing includes company directors.

### 1.4 KEBS — Kenya Bureau of Standards
- **Main site:** https://www.kebs.org
- **Diamond Mark (D-Mark) Certification:** Voluntary product certification scheme
- **Certified companies:** Search for companies with KEBS Diamond Mark for electrical/solar products
- **Directory:** Contact KEBS for certified company lists in specific product categories

**Action:** Companies with KEBS certification for electrical equipment or solar panels are established players worth targeting.

### 1.5 Kenya Gazette
- **Official Gazette:** https://www.governmentpress.go.ke/the-kenya-gazette-
  - Weekly publication of all government notices
- **What's relevant:** Company incorporations, name changes, EPRA license notices, deregistrations
- **Alternative access:** Check Scribd for recent gazette PDFs or Google "Kenya Gazette [month] [year]"

**Action:** Search gazette notices for EPRA license issuances — new licensed companies appear here before they show up on EPRA's website.

### 1.6 Business Registration Service (BRS)
- **Main site:** https://brs.go.ke
- **Company search via eCitizen:** https://ecitizen.go.ke → Business Registration Service
  - Search any registered company in Kenya
  - Costs KES 650 per search
  - Returns CR12 documents showing directors, shareholders, registration details

**Action:** Use eCitizen BRS to verify company legitimacy and get director information for companies you discover elsewhere.

### 1.7 County Government Procurement Portals
- Each county publishes tender awards and contract notices
- Search: `"[county name] county government tender awards electrical"` or `site:[county website] tenders`
- Example: Nairobi City County, Mombasa County, Nakuru County

## 2. INDUSTRY ASSOCIATIONS

### 2.1 KEREA — Kenya Renewable Energy Association
- **Website:** https://kerea.org
- **What they do:** Independent non-profit association dedicated to facilitating growth and development of renewable energy in Kenya
- **Members:** Solar companies, wind developers, hydro developers, off-grid companies
- **GOGLA membership:** https://members.gogla.org/members/kenya-renewable-energy-association/

**Action:** Contact KEREA for their member directory. Attend their events to meet solar/renewable companies.

### 2.2 KAM — Kenya Association of Manufacturers
- **Website:** https://www.kam.co.ke
- **What they do:** Representative body of manufacturing and value-added industries in Kenya (est. 1959)
- **Members:** Industrial companies, equipment manufacturers, energy users

**Action:** KAM has sector-specific committees. Ask about the energy/environment committee members.

### 2.3 KEPSA — Kenya Private Sector Alliance
- **Website:** https://www.kepsa.or.ke
- **What they do:** Premier umbrella body representing over 2 million businesses in Kenya
- **Members:** Multinationals to startups across all sectors

**Action:** KEPSA has sector-specific working groups. Inquire about energy/infrastructure sector members.

### 2.4 ESAK — Electricity Sector Association of Kenya
- **LinkedIn:** https://ke.linkedin.com/company/electricity-sector-association-of-kenya-esak
- **Twitter:** @ESA_Kenya
- **What they do:** Member-based org of IPPs, project developers, consultants, contractors, legal practitioners, finance organisations across Generation, Transmission & Distribution, and Off-Grid

**Action:** ESAK represents private power producers and developers — exactly the target sector. Contact them for member directory.

### 2.5 AFSIA — Africa Solar Industry Association
- **Website:** https://www.afsiasolar.com
- **Membership Directory:** Available on their site
- **What they do:** Leading business association representing Africa's solar industry

**Action:** Search their membership directory for Kenya-based solar companies. Many Kenyan solar EPCs are members.

### 2.6 GOGLA — Global Off-Grid Lighting Association
- **Website:** https://gogla.org
- **Member Directory:** https://gogla.org/members
- **What they do:** Global association for off-grid solar energy solutions
- **Kenya connection:** Many GOGLA members operate in Kenya

**Action:** Filter members by Kenya. These are off-grid solar companies — pay-as-you-go, solar home systems, mini-grids.

### 2.7 EEE Kenya — Electrical Engineering in Kenya
- **Website:** https://www.eeekenya.com
- **Solar contractors list:** https://www.eeekenya.com/solar-systems-contractors-in-kenya/
- **Contractor register download:** https://www.eeekenya.com/downloads/updated-register-of-licensed-electrical-contractors/

**Action:** This is a goldmine. The site maintains lists of solar contractors, electrical contractors, and has downloadable registers with contact details.

### 2.8 KABCEC — Kenya Association of Building and Civil Engineering Contractors
- **Website:** https://kabcec.co.ke
- **Members:** Over 400 employer organizations — top contractors in Kenya (est. 1949)

**Action:** Many KABCEC members also do electrical works. Request their member directory.

## 4. CONTACT FINDING WITHOUT HUNTER/SKRAPP

### 4.1 Google Dorking — Specific Queries

Use these search queries to find direct contact information:

**Finding company emails:**
```
"@[companydomain].co.ke" email
"contact@[companydomain].co.ke"
"info@[companydomain].co.ke"
site:[companydomain].co.ke "@"
```

**Finding technical team contacts:**
```
"electrical engineer" site:linkedin.com Kenya -linkedin.com (finds indexed profiles)
"[company name]" "engineering manager" Kenya
"[company name]" "project manager" electrical Kenya
```

**Finding WhatsApp Business numbers:**
```
"[company name]" WhatsApp Kenya
"solar company" WhatsApp Nairobi
"electrical contractor" WhatsApp Nairobi
```

**Finding company documents with contact details:**
```
"[company name]" filetype:pdf tender
"[company name]" filetype:pdf proposal
"[company name]" filetype:pdf annual report
```

### 4.2 Company Website Scraping Patterns

Most Kenyan company websites follow these URL patterns for contact info:
- `/contact-us` or `/contact`
- `/about-us` or `/about`
- `/our-team` or `/team`
- `/careers` or `/jobs`

**Email patterns for Kenyan companies:**
- `info@company.co.ke`
- `admin@company.co.ke`
- `careers@company.co.ke`
- `sales@company.co.ke`
- `projects@company.co.ke`
- `firstname.lastname@company.co.ke`

### 4.3 Phone-Based Discovery

**Strategy:** Call the company's front desk number (found on Google Maps) and ask:
1. "Hi, I'm calling to inquire about your [solar/electrical/automation] services. Could I speak to someone in your technical or engineering department?"
2. "I'd like to send over my CV for consideration. Could you give me the email address for your HR department or engineering manager?"
3. "Are you currently hiring electrical engineers? Who should I send my application to?"

**Tips:**
- Call between 9-11 AM or 2-4 PM when decision-makers are likely at their desks
- Be professional and direct about your intent
- Kenyan companies are generally responsive to phone inquiries
- Ask for the name AND email of the person you're referred to

### 4.4 WhatsApp Business Discovery

- Many Kenyan solar and electrical companies list WhatsApp Business numbers on Google Maps
- Send a professional introduction message:
  > "Hello [Company Name], I'm Wilber — an electrical engineer with experience in power generation (KenGen Olkaria geothermal, Kiambere Hydro SCADA/HV). I'm exploring opportunities in [solar/automation/power]. Would you be open to a brief conversation about potential openings or collaboration?"
- WhatsApp is preferred over email for initial contact in Kenya
